Characters

Mike Ross - Brilliant fraud with photographic memory. He does not have a law degree yet continues practicing law. Values justice, loyalty, and love. Truth stands in an endless battle against his commitment to ambition.

Harvey Spectre - As a top corporate lawyer this professional displays intense self-confidence alongside keen intelligence. Wins cases with style and wit. His loyalty extends only to a handful of people and he uses an arrogant behavior to conceal his genuine emotional responses. Loves Donna, hates losing.

Louise Litt - This peculiar person seeks social recognition along with self-worth. I have three main interests: law along with cats and mudding activities. Insecure but brilliant. A pure heart exists underneath her explosive emotions and her shy behavior.

Jessica Pearson - The managing partner of the firm stands out with powerful presence while retaining full command over all matters. Balances legal brilliance with personal sacrifice. Protects firm fiercely. Respected, feared, and admired. Always ten steps ahead.

Rachele Zane - Ambitious paralegal turned lawyer. Her drive along with determination makes her aim to move forward as an independent individual who demonstrates both mental and physical expertise. She stands behind Mike and recognizes both moral values along with finding equilibrium between her romantic bonds and professional growth.

Donna Paulsen - Harvey trusted this person to work both as his confidante and his brilliant assistant. Emotionally intelligent, fearless, and intuitive. Moves from behind the desk to COO. The individual aligns with principles of love and loyalty combined with power beliefs.
